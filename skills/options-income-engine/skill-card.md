## Description: <br>
Options Income Engine helps agents produce options-income strategy guidance for premium collection, including iron condors, cash-secured puts, covered calls, calendar spreads, backtests, and volatility-informed strike and expiration analysis. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Options traders and trading-focused agents use this skill to draft income-oriented options strategies, compare strikes and expirations, and frame backtests and market-condition checks for user review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Options strategy output may be financially consequential if treated as trade instructions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as educational strategy drafts and require user review before any trading decision. <br>
Risk: Backtests, market-condition analysis, and strike selection may be inaccurate or stale without verified data. <br>
Mitigation: Use verified market data and independently review calculations before relying on the output. <br>
