## Use Case: <br>
External users and traders use this skill for general trading education, technical analysis, chart pattern review, risk management calculations, position sizing, and trade-planning analysis without personalized financial advice. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users could mistake educational trading analysis for financial advice or direct trade recommendations. <br>
Mitigation: Require legal disclaimer acknowledgement, frame outputs as analysis rather than advice, avoid buy/sell imperatives and personalized suitability claims, and remind users that they control all decisions. <br>
Risk: Optional local notes under ~/trading/ could contain sensitive financial details if users over-share. <br>
Mitigation: Use the notes only for learning preferences and progress; avoid broker credentials, account numbers, detailed holdings, and private financial records. <br>
Risk: Trading and leveraged products can produce substantial losses despite technical analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Emphasize risk management, position sizing, stop losses, and the limits of technical analysis before strategy discussion. <br>
