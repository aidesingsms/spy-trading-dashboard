## Description: <br>
Builds and runs Python options strategy backtests for strategies such as iron condors, strangles, calendar spreads, and vertical credit spreads, with reported metrics including Sharpe ratio, win rate, max drawdown, expectancy, and equity curve. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may present randomized demo results as historical options backtesting, which could mislead users making trading decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as synthetic demonstrations unless the implementation is corrected to use validated market data, strategy-specific logic, and enforced risk filters. <br>
Risk: Finance-related outputs may be mistaken for investment advice or reliable strategy-performance evidence. <br>
Mitigation: Require human review by qualified users before using outputs for trading, risk management, or strategy evaluation. <br>
